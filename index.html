Our Story

Canid Canines: Pixel Odyssey

The Alpha

For as long as anyone could remember, members of the “Canid” family of species, namely foxes and wolves, lived peacefully and in harmony deep within a forest. A few human villages were scattered around the edge of this forest. As human civilization advanced over time, so an industrial-era ensued; villages became cities, and the forest became a dumping-ground for radioactive waste, causing the extinction of most species.

The Genesis

The radioactive waste caused a mutation in the DNA of some Canid youngsters who had been left behind in their dens (1110 of them to be precise). As chance and biology would have it, this caused them to develop human-like attributes and intelligence. Over the years, the young Canids studied human books, science and technology, culminating in the construction of the first Canid village. Meanwhile, humans waged nuclear war amongst themselves which brought their own mass-extinction.

The Odyssey

As the humans decreased, so life began to flourish once again in the forest. However, much of the food and water supply was still contaminated by radiation. Canids managed to identify the source to be multiple vestigial nuclear power-stations, left unattended by the humans. By this point, Canids are running low on reserves. Resources are scarce - they need to migrate!
